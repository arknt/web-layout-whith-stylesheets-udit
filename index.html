Critical Reflection

ATELIER Reflection

Through this comparative exercise, I recognized how runtime environments (Node.js, Ruby, or standalone) shape both development flow and complexity. Node.js tools like Tailwind and Shadcn offer flexibility and modularity but demand constant updates and dependency management. Meanwhile, standalone solutions such as Bootstrap or Hugo promote simplicity and stability at the cost of some customization.

My preferred workflow balances speed and control: Hugo for structure, TailwindCSS for design systems, and GSAP for motion. This combination reflects my value of minimal dependencies with maximum creative expression.
